Skate Spots

Skate Spot 1

Trinity Skate Spot

Trinity Skate Spot is located behind the Trinity Rep. and Brewhouse. It is down a one way sidestreet at 201 Washington St, Providence, RI 02903. It was originally just a regular skate spot, very rough, cracks and broken brick everywhere. It was still a dope spot, however it was tough to skate because all of the cracks and chunks missing in the ground. However, it was turned into an official Safe Skate Spot and was renovated by the city and officially opened by the Governor of Providence. All of this makes this OG spot even more ledgendary.

Trinity SkateSpot

Trinity Skatespot is a perfect warm up spot right in the middle of downtown. It is a rennovated skate spot so its a good place to ride smooth ground before adventuring the rough streets of Providence

Skate Spot 2

Granny Banks

The Granny Banks is also an OG spot of Providence Rhode Island. The reason behind its name is because the way skateboarding is there. Years back, you would only have a certain amount of time to skate the spots because the location is actually an apartment and office buildings. Located at 457 Westminister St. Back in the day if you skateboarded at the spot for too long the "old ladies" would start to yell or even throw water out there windows above the spot.

Old GrannyBanks

This spot is a good spot to venture off too after warming up. It is easy to get too and also for skaters off all levels. You can do wallrides, skate the stairs, or get gnarly and skate the gap.

Skate Spot 3

Providence Monument

The Providence monument is an extremely popular spot for locals and skatebaorders visiting the city. It is an aesthetically pleasing spot when recording tricks. Also, it is a perfect spot for skateboarders of all difficulties. It is located at: Memorial Park College St and Main Street Providence, RI USA 02903

Providence Monument

The Providence Monument is a great spot to go to if you are first venturing the city. It is deeper into the city and is perfect for skaters of all levels. It is also very close to other spots.

Skate Spot 4

RISD Ledges

The RISD Ledges is a perfect spot for skateboarding and also hanging out and enjoying the view. In the summer, boat's will be going by in the water, also the architecture looks so aesthetically pleasing if you're looking for places to film. The ledges arent too high and actually moderately low to an amateur skateboarder, making it a perfect place to learn tricks and perfect your own ones. The RISD Ledges can be found at the canal walk in front of RISD and the water.

RISD Ledges

RISD ledges is a spot that can accomodate all skaters. The ledges aren'too high and are buttery for skating. The ledges are also right next to the river making it an ideal filming spot.

    U Melt

    Bright counter-serve spot turning out a variety of grilled cheese sandwiches, plus soups & salads. Umelt can be found at 129 Weybosset St, Providence, RI 02903 right across from the Johnson & Wales University.

    Olneyville N.Y. System

    Casual space for hot dogs topped with specialty meat sauce & traditional eats such as french fries and burgers. This food spot is good and convenient if you're skating the ledges at Bridgham or just down exploring Olneyville. This food spot is located at 18 Plainfield St, Providence, RI 02909.

    Antonio's Pizzeria

    Casual pizzeria chain serving traditional, thin-crust & Sicilian pies, plus wings, pasta & subs. Antonios is a perfect place to go get a big diversity in pizza. The slices are big and stray away from the traditional cheese and pepperoni however you can get that if you'd like. Antonio's Pizza is located on, 256 Thayer St, Providence, RI 02906 and it is a great place to stop by for good food while skating down the hill of Thayer Street.

    Insomnia Cookies

    Insomnia Cookies Providence is located on one of the most popular streets in town. It is directly across from Brown University and is a few blocks away from Meehan Auditorium at 307 Thayer Street. Insomnia cookies is a cookie shop that bakes delicious cookies of all different flavors. It also offeres ice cream cookie sandwiches. Perfect spot to finish the day after a long hard day of skateboarding or exploring.

    Free Play Bar & Arcade

    This place is as good as it gets. Combining the concepts of a bar and an arcade together. Great crowds and friendly atmosphere. This bar is a unique nightlife type of arcade bar in Providence R.I.The drinks are good and reasonably priced and bartenders are nice and responsive. The cover charge is reasonable too, $5 to play for hours on end. Or $10 on Friday and Saturday's. Definitely a perfect place to spend your nights while visiting Providence. Located at 182 Pine St, Providence, RI 02903.
